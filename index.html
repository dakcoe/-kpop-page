The Evolution of K-POP

From 90s hip-hop roots to a 21st-century global powerhouse.

1990s

1st Gen

The birth of modern K-pop with Seo Taiji and Boys. Establishing the idol blueprint with H.O.T. and S.E.S.

2000s

2nd Gen

The "Korean Wave" (Hallyu) spreads across Asia. High production and synchronized dance by TVXQ and Girls' Generation.

2010s

3rd Gen

Global explosion via social media. BTS and BLACKPINK become worldwide phenomena.

Late 2010s+

4th Gen

Global-first strategies. Experimental sounds and international focus by NewJeans and Stray Kids.

2023+

5th Gen

Rapid global engagement and hyper-connected fandoms led by groups like Zerobaseone.
